In 1990, Tim Berners-Lee develops HTML, which made a huge contribution to how we navigate and view the Internet today.
Tim Berners-Lee introduces WWW to the public on August 6,1991 and becomes available for everyone August 23,1991.
The World Wide Web (WWW) is what most people today consider the "Internet" or a series of sites and pages that are connected with links.
The Internet had hundreds of people who helped develop the standards and technologies used today.
Without the WWW, the Internet would not be as popular as it is today.
